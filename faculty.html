MY FACULTY

INTRODUCTION:

Dalian Polytechnic University, located in Liaoning Province, was founded in 1958. As of May 2021, the school has 49 undergraduate majors, 3 doctoral degree authorized first-level disciplines, 2 post-doctoral research stations, 14 master's degree authorized first-level disciplines, and 8 master's degree authorized categories. There are more than 850 full-time teachers, 13,000 undergraduate students, and 3,000 graduate students.
