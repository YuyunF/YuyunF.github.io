CITY OF DALIAN


Brief Introduction:

Key words:Sea; Snow; Romance

Dalian is a seaside city of seven million people, which located in the northeast of China and is known as the "Pearl of the North" and the "Capital of Romance". 

It is an important economic, financial, trade, port, industrial and tourist city on the northern coast of China. 
It is one of the five planned cities, one of the 15 sub-provincial cities, and one of the 14 coastal open cities in the country. 
At the same time, Dalian is also the international shipping center, international logistics center and regional financial center in Northeast China.

Dalian has a mild climate with four distinct seasons and humid air. 
    

It is home to the largest square in Asia, ''Xinghai Square'', which means Coast of Stars. 

Dalian was famous for its football culture. Dalian hosts an international marathon and an international beer festival every year.
